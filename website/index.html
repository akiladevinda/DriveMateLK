FAQ

Questions drivers ask

What is DriveMate LK?

DriveMate LK is an intelligent vehicle companion for Sri Lanka — combining AI fault guidance, fuel & expense tracking, reminders, documents and nearby garages in one app.

Is Scan Fault a confirmed diagnosis?

No. Scan Fault provides general guidance only. Always confirm with a qualified mechanic when safety is uncertain.

Which vehicles are supported?

Phase one focuses on Car, Van, Motor Bike and 3 Wheel — with Sri Lanka–relevant makes and models.

Do I need an account?

Yes. Creating an account keeps your vehicles, documents and history synced securely.

Where can I download it?

App Store and Google Play links will appear in the download section once listings go live.
